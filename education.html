OU "Bratstvo-Migjeni" from Tetovo is one of the oldest, founded in 1958 under the name "Bratstvo - edinstvo". It was and still is located on the street "Ljubo Bozhinoski - Pish" no. 41. In it, teaching was first conducted in the Macedonian and Albanian languages from the first to the eighth grade.

Gymnasium Kiril Pejčinoviḱ" is a gymnasium in Tetovo, North Macedonia. It was established in 1947 and named after the enlightener Kiril Pejčinoviḱ.

The Faculty of Information Sciences and Computer Engineering (FINKI) is the result of the merger of the two largest institutes - the Institute of Informatics at the Faculty of Science and Mathematics and the Institute of Computer Technology and Informatics at the Faculty of Electrical Engineering.
